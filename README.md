# Liza the Lizard: Virtual Excavation Game & Cultural Heritage (2006)

An interactive educational game designed for children aged 10–12, developed as part of a Master’s Thesis in Cultural Informatics at the University of Aegean. The project aims to raise awareness about cultural heritage preservation and counter-antiquities trafficking.

### 🏛️ Exhibition & Academic Background
This application was featured in the international exhibition **"The Theft of History"**, supported by the EU **"CULTURE 2000"** program. It was showcased at the National Archaeological Museum of Cyprus, the Benaki Museum (Athens), and the Archaeological Museum of Corinth.

**Project Partners Included:**
* University of the Aegean (Dept. of Cultural Technology & Communication)
* ANEMON Productions
* Illicit Antiquities Research Centre (University of Cambridge, UK)
* 37th Ephorate of Prehistoric & Classical Antiquities (Corinth)
* Department of Antiquities of Cyprus
